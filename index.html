KEY LOCATIONS of the Lunar Islands

Name Features Population General Rating
The Starcrest Lake The heart of the Inner Forest, and home to many aquatic species who prefer freshwater environments to the salty shores. It is said to resemble the infinite galaxies when the stars reflect upon it's surface come midnight. ~ 10 8/10

Not suitable for those who can't swim, but it's a wonderful gathering/ sight seeing place for forest dwellers alike.
GreenWood Forest Northernmost territory of LUNAR with lot's of unique areas like the Crystal Falls, Flower Fields, abundant Forest Cabins and views like no other. Nestled between two towering mountains, travel is hard but it also keeps danger away, making for a great home. ~ 60 10/10

It's hard to beat for sure, you have plenty of room to breath while always having a friendly neighbore to turn to. Nature surrounds you at every corner and creek.
Plains The outer areas to the east of the Greenwood Forest most like to adventure into. It's new while not posing too many dangers. Some thing's to look out for though are The Pit, The Haunted Woodlands, and vaious minor hideouts of rogue creatures. ~ 13 7/10

Fun for the few who make it home, either with a group or on their own, but a few were also not so fortunate...
Outskirts The area to the west of the forest is less touresty and earned a repuation of crime and unrest due to it's proximity to the lawless lands of the desert. A traveling van can be spotted from time to time, it's intentions are unknown. ~ 4 5/10

Pretty boring ngl, unless your the guy driving the van or something, they look like they're having fun
Tree Trunk Cove Before you see land, you spot a giant leefy platform supported by a thick winded trunk. The Tree of Life. It's a cozy remote location, the residents live in towering hollow tree; taking care of the magnifiscient structure and protecting from harm. ~ 2 7/10

While you can't just live there (the 2 guardians of the tree are carefully selected for life); for the two that do, the tree is a good enough home for them.
The Ruins Behind the Lorelian Palace rests what remains of a once bustling city. Whetever happened to it? Who remains that call the ruins home do what they can to survive. ~ 9 4/10

The air sucks, the land sucks, and there's barely any food left to scourage. However, it's geography make's it almost impossible for the common folk to esape.
The Desert It's a desert...nothing special... nothing at all worth hiding or anything ~ 0 2/10

Yeah you can't survive here alone
Stardew Valley gasp I didn't know Pelican Town of Stardew Valley of the Ferngil Republic was actually on LUNAR?!?!?! wowee ~ 85 11/10 would live there yup please get me there immediately please and thank you
